Green Goggles

Green Goggles

The Brief

How can Husqvarna extend its role from outdoor equipment leader to active contributor to societal wellbeing?

The Solution

We identified a growing problem: nursing homes often lack access to green environments. At the same time, research consistently shows that exposure to nature reduces stress, lowers blood pressure, and improves overall wellbeing. Yet the people who need nature the most, the elderly, the immobile, the vulnerable, are the ones least able to access it. Green Goggles brings nature inside. By using immersive technology, Husqvarna transforms indoor spaces into restorative green environments, creating the world’s greenest therapy room and restoring access to nature when physical access is no longer possible.
